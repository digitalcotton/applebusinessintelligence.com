Apple Introduces Groundbreaking Business Intelligence Platform, in Collaboration with Microsoft and OpenAI

CUPERTINO, California——Apple® today announced the debut of Apple Business Intelligence, an innovative platform that combines Apple's hallmark design, intuitive user experience, and stringent privacy standards with the power of Microsoft's enterprise tools and OpenAI's state-of-the-art artificial intelligence models. This new suite is set to redefine the way organizations analyze and visualize their data.

Reimagining Business Intelligence

Apple Business Intelligence harnesses the immersive capabilities of Apple Vision Pro™ to create a visually stunning and interactive environment for data exploration. By integrating cutting-edge AI from OpenAI and leveraging advanced enterprise features from Microsoft, this platform empowers organizations to generate deeper, more actionable insights from their information in real time—all within Apple's secure and seamless ecosystem.

Apple Design Ethos Meets Advanced AI

Continuing Apple's legacy of intuitive interfaces and user-centric design, Apple Business Intelligence provides a sleek, approachable framework for teams of all sizes. Enhanced by OpenAI's advanced language and data models, the platform offers intelligent recommendations, automated data trend analysis, and powerful collaboration tools, eliminating the need for complex scripts and queries. Business users, data analysts, and executives alike can visualize and interact with their data in entirely new ways, thanks to the rich spatial computing environment of Apple Vision Pro.

Collaboration with Microsoft

Built with Microsoft's leading enterprise solutions in mind, Apple Business Intelligence integrates seamlessly with services such as Microsoft 365® and Azure®, enabling users to quickly import, transform, and share data across their existing workflows. This collaboration ensures that organizations can leverage the strength of Microsoft's robust enterprise ecosystem, alongside Apple's commitment to security, privacy, and user experience.

Expanding the Apple Ecosystem

From the moment a user opens Apple Business Intelligence on an iPad® or Mac®, they enter a unified space that effortlessly syncs with their broader Apple environment. Teams can securely share live data reports or immersive dashboards across devices. Combined with Apple's strong stance on data protection—including on-device processing for sensitive information—this platform ensures that every piece of information remains secure and under the user's control.

"Business Intelligence is entering a new era," said Tim Cook, Apple's CEO. "By blending Apple's core principles of privacy and design with the unparalleled power of Vision Pro, Microsoft's enterprise tools, and OpenAI's advanced AI models, we're offering businesses a truly transformative way to make informed decisions and spark innovation."

Availability

The Apple Business Intelligence platform is available in select markets now, with global rollout scheduled for 2026. Customers can preview the Apple Business Intelligence Case Study to learn more.
